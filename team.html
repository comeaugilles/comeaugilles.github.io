Gilles is a senior IT professional with 13 years of engineering and project management experience focused on the delivery of reliable online services at massive scale and complexity. Gilles has a proven track record of solving business problems using emerging technology solutions and evaluating the impact of new technologies on business strategy and direction. Before becoming CTO of Polecat, Gilles managed the implementation of Azure for MSN in Microsoft, and prior to that managed the integration of several acquisitions into the Online Services Division at MS. Gilles holds an MBA from a top 25 FT ranked university: Grenoble Graduate School of Business, a Bachelor of Computer Science from UNB, and many industry recognized certifications including PMP, ITIL Master, MCPD, OCP 8/9, ISO 20000 and 27001.
